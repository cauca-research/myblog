## Who I Am

After failing to make a living as a photographer, I pivoted toward a career in IT and cybersecurity — a natural step, as I’ve always been drawn to technology. The journey has been both rewarding and challenging.

## Certifications

Over the past five years, I’ve earned several industry certifications:
- **CompTIA A+** -- foundational IT knowledge
- **Cisco CyberOps Associate** -- SOC operations and security monitoring
- **CCNA** -- routing, switching, and network fundamentals
- **CISSP** -- security architecture, risk management, and governance
- **OSCP** *(in progress)* -- hands-on offensive security and penetration testing

## What I Do

My career spans roles across IT support, GRC, information security and pentesting. I've worked in environments ranging from small businesses to large enterprises, and I bring a builder's mindset to security -- understanding the infrastructure makes me better at defending it.

Current focus areas:
- Penetration testing and offensive security
- AI and social engineering 
- Homelab experimentation and continuous learning
