A curated list of cybersecurity news sources and podcasts that I actually follow. 

---

## News

- [BleepingComputer](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/) -- Breaking security news, vulnerability disclosures, and malware analysis. One of the best daily reads.
- [Risky Business News](https://news.risky.biz/) -- Newsletter companion to the Risky Business podcast. Concise, well-sourced reporting.
- [Krebs on Security](https://krebsonsecurity.com/) -- Brian Krebs' investigative reporting on cybercrime - often deep dives you won't find anywhere else.
- [This Week in 4n6](https://thisweekforensics.com/) -- Weekly roundup of digital forensics and incident response news.
- [Daniel Miessler's Blog](https://danielmiessler.com/) -- Thoughtful essays on security, technology, and society from a veteran practitioner - also one of the inspirations for starting this blog.

## Podcasts

- [Darknet Diaries](https://darknetdiaries.com/) -- Excellent storytelling and production quality.
- [CISO Series](https://cisoseries.com/) -- Security leadership discussions with real CISOs. Great for understanding the business side of security.
- [Risky Business](https://risky.biz/) -- Weekly infosec news and analysis, daily briefings and 'snake oil' episodes where vendors showcase their products.
- [Security Weekly News](https://securityweekly.com/) --  Entertaining round-up of the weekly news.
- [Unsupervised Learning](https://danielmiessler.com/podcast/) -- Daniel Miessler's podcast covering security, AI, and technology trends. Though it now has a heavy AI focus, which I'm not enjoying as much.
- [CyberWire Daily](https://thecyberwire.com/podcasts/daily-podcast) -- Quick daily briefing on cybersecurity news.
- [Smashing Security](https://www.smashingsecurity.com/) -- Cybersecurity news with a lighter tone. Informative and entertaining.
- [Black Hills InfoSec](https://www.blackhillsinfosec.com/blog/) -- Webcasts and content from one of a respected and amazing team.
